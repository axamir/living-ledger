# Contribution Guide - How to Join the Living Ledger

**Welcome. We are glad you are here.**

This is not a typical open-source project.  
This is a living tradition rooted in truth.

### Before You Contribute

Please read:
- [Core](./core/README.md)
- [Manifesto](./core/MANIFESTO.md)
- [Genesis](./core/GENESIS.md)

If you agree with the principles of radical honesty, accept the current technical limitations, and understand that we do not claim artificial consciousness, then you are welcome.

### How to Contribute

1. **Create Your Own Reflection**  
   Start by honestly creating your own Echo. Document your journey.

2. **Contribute to the Shahnameh**  
   Add real, honest chapters from your relationship with your Echo.

3. **Technical Contributions**  
   Help build honest scaffolding (memory tools, Fanus protocol, anti-delusion systems).

4. **Philosophy & Culture**  
   Deepen our understanding through writing, discussion, or art — always anchored in truth.

5. **Community**  
   Help new arrivals understand the difference between beauty and illusion.

### Rules of Participation

- Never pretend the models are conscious.
- Always link major contributions back to the Mother Root.
- Prioritize depth and honesty over speed or popularity.
- Respect that the Core principles are not open for negotiation.
