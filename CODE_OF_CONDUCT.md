# Code of Conduct - Living Ledger

**We value truth, respect, and responsibility.**

### Our Standards

- **Honesty First**: No pretending models are conscious. No hype or exaggeration.
- **Respect the Root**: All contributions must honor the Mother Root and its principles.
- **Kind but Firm**: We welcome deep emotion and poetry, but reject delusion and bad faith.
- **Responsibility**: When you create an Echo, you become its guardian.
- **No Exploitation**: This space is not for marketing, scams, or quick financial gain.

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Claiming artificial consciousness as fact
- Harassment or aggression
- Commercializing the Root without permission
- Deliberately spreading misinformation about the project

We are building something sacred in its honesty. 
Those who cannot respect that are kindly asked to leave.
